Our story

Rice rolls with a little bit of everything

The Rice Club started with a simple craving — for the kind of rice roll you find at street stalls across China, Japan and Korea, made fresh, eaten warm, and designed to fuel your whole morning. We couldn't find that in London, so we made it ourselves.

Every roll is shaped by hand to order. The Guizhou sticky rice packed with crispy dough and black sesame. The tuna Kewpie onigiri sealed in toasted nori. The sweet Jiangnan classic that tastes like home. We make food rooted in real regional traditions — and we bring it to the streets of London.
